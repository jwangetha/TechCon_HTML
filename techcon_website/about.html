Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of innovators passionate about shaping the future of technology. Since then, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global conferences, attracting thousands of attendees each year.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire, educate, and connect technology enthusiasts, developers, and leaders from around the globe. We aim to foster innovation, spark collaboration, and create opportunities for future breakthroughs.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer, Dr. Lovelace’s legacy continues to inspire innovation in computing. Her keynote emphasized the creative potential of technology.

Alan Turing

A pioneer of computer science and artificial intelligence, Alan Turing’s groundbreaking ideas laid the foundation for modern computing.

Grace Hopper

A visionary computer scientist and naval officer, Grace Hopper revolutionized programming with her work on compilers and early programming languages.
